Our Philosophy

Learning Beyond Books

At Acacia Primary, we believe education extends far beyond the classroom walls. Our extensive extracurricular programme offers students opportunities to explore new interests, develop talents, and build friendships across grade levels.

From creative arts to STEM clubs, sports to community service, there's something for every child to discover their passion and shine.
