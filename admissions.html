Quality Education for All

Kwamtshwili Primary School is a Quintile 1 no-fee school, providing free education to all learners in our community. We believe every child deserves access to quality education regardless of their family's financial situation.

As a designated no-fee school, we receive government funding to cover operational costs. Our learners also benefit from the National School Nutrition Programme, receiving daily meals at school.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

When do applications open?

Applications for the following academic year open in March and close in October. Late applications may be considered based on availability.

Do you offer scholarships?

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships for exceptional students and bursaries for families demonstrating financial need.

What is the school day schedule?

School runs from 7:30 AM to 3:30 PM, Monday through Friday. Early arrival supervision is available from 7:00 AM.

Do you accept mid-year transfers?

Mid-year admissions are considered based on space availability in the relevant grade level. Please contact our admissions office.
