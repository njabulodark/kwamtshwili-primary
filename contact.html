FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a school tour?

You can schedule a tour by calling our office at +260 123 456 789, emailing admissions@acaciaprimary.ac.zm, or using the contact form above. Tours are available Monday through Friday during school hours.

What is the best time to contact the school?

Our office is open from 7:30 AM to 4:00 PM on weekdays. The best times to reach us are between 8:00-10:00 AM or 2:00-4:00 PM when administrative staff are most available.

Do you offer virtual meetings for prospective parents?

Yes, we offer virtual meetings via Zoom or Google Meet for parents who cannot visit in person. Please mention this in your inquiry and we'll arrange a convenient time.

How quickly can I expect a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during school days. For urgent matters, please call our office directly.
