Our Approach

National Curriculum (CAPS)

Kwamtshwili Primary School follows the South African National Curriculum Statement (NCS) and Curriculum and Assessment Policy Statement (CAPS), ensuring our learners meet national standards.

Our Foundation Phase emphasizes mother-tongue (isiZulu) literacy instruction, while the Intermediate Phase transitions learners to English as a First Additional Language (EFAL).

Assessment

Tracking Progress

We use a variety of assessment methods to monitor student progress and inform teaching. Our approach balances formal examinations with ongoing formative assessment.

1

Formative Assessment

Ongoing checks for understanding through quizzes, observations, and classwork.

2

Summative Assessment

End-of-term examinations and projects that evaluate cumulative learning.

3

Progress Reports

Detailed termly reports with grades and teacher comments for parents.
