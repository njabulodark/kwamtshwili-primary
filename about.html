About Kwamtshwili Primary

A Quintile 1 no-fee school serving the Kwamtshwili Reserve community in Nkandla, KwaZulu-Natal.

Who We Are

A Community of Resilience

Kwamtshwili Primary School (EMIS: 500181892) is a public ordinary primary school serving learners from Grade R to Grade 7 in the heart of KwaZulu-Natal's King Cetshwayo District.

Our 470 learners are supported by dedicated teachers and staff committed to delivering quality education through the CAPS curriculum, despite the challenges of our rural, Quintile 1 context.

Student Experience

Life at Acacia

At Acacia Primary, every student is known, valued, and encouraged to reach their full potential. Our supportive community fosters academic excellence, personal growth, and lifelong friendships.

From the moment students arrive each morning to the end of the school day, they are engaged in a rich tapestry of learning experiences that prepare them for success.
