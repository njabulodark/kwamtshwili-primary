Our Story

About Kwamtshwili Primary

A Quintile 1 no-fee school serving the Kwamtshwili Reserve community in Nkandla, KwaZulu-Natal.

Our Mission

Nurturing Tomorrow's Leaders

Kwamtshwili Primary School is a public primary school serving learners from Grade R to Grade 7 in the King Cetshwayo District of KwaZulu-Natal.

We deliver the CAPS curriculum with dedication, fostering academic excellence, cultural pride, and community values in every learner.
